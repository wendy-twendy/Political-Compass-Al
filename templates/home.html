Welcome to the Political Compass Quiz

The Political Compass Quiz is designed to help you understand your political beliefs by placing you on a two-dimensional grid. This grid represents economic views (left to right) and social views (libertarian to authoritarian).

Answer 20 questions about various political issues, and we'll plot your position on the political compass. This can help you better understand your own political leanings and how they compare to different political ideologies.
